Why?

The Traction Tracker is designed to help start-ups and founders keep track of their early Customer Development activities.

From initial Target Customer to Minimum Viable Product (MVP) and Product-Market Fit (PMF), the Traction Tracker helps ensure your time is spent reducing risks and validating hypotheses instead of wasting time building a business around fundamentally flawed assumptions.

The main cause of start-up failure is not listening to customers and lack of focus and our intention is to help founders and start-ups increase their odds of success by spending the early discovery stages of starting something focused on Customer Development.
